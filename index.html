Welcome to Brookwood Guitars!

Family-owned And Operated

In 1998, Ray Brookwood founded Brookwood Guitars with the help of his brother Ken and sister Mary. Today, the three siblings are still just as passionate about the company and even more involved than ever before.

Excellent Sound

World renowned for their full, balanced expression, warm bass and excellent projection, every Brookwood Guitar has been refined to carry this legacy to new heights.

The Finest Materials

Whether you are repairing a guitar or customizing one, you want parts that sound and look good. Brookwood Guitars has been crafting boutique-quality guitar and bass parts for over 20 years. We offer an unrivaled range of options on our necks and bodies, including standard and exotic woods, pickup and bridge routs, neck profiles, frets, inlays, binding, and more. Make Brookwood Guitars your source for custom bass and guitar parts.
